About Us

After a brief time running a bikers cafe in Rhyl called The Rumbling Tum, we noticed due to popularity that we were going to need more space.

So, we are proud to present to you, Unit 1 Cafe MCC, based in Unit 1 Bayside, Kinmel Bay.

Large parking facilities directly outside, Jonesie's Motorcycles (the local specialists) are directly the opposite side of the carpark for all your bike needs.

Delivery available, just request on ordering.
